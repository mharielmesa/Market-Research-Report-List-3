<p><p>Neurodiagnostic and monitoring devices market includes Electroencephalogram (EEG), Electromyography (EMG), Transcranial Doppler, Intracranial pressure monitor, and Cerebral oximeters. EEG devices measure brain wave patterns, while EMG devices evaluate muscle function. Transcranial Doppler devices assess blood flow in the brain. Intracranial pressure monitors measure pressure within the skull. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in the brain. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring neurological conditions, helping healthcare professionals provide appropriate treatment and care for patients.</p></p>
