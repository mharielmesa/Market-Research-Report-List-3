Saturable core NSFCLs use magnetic material to control current flow, while solid state NSFCLs utilize semiconductor technology for the same purpose. Both types offer benefits such as improved system stability and reduced equipment damage in the event of a fault, making them essential components in modern electrical grids.</p></p>
