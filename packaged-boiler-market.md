<p><p>Packaged boilers are categorized into two main types; Shell Boilers and Drum Boilers. Shell boilers consist of a large cylindrical vessel, known as the shell, where hot gases pass through tubes filled with water to generate steam. Drum boilers, on the other hand, have a drum or vessel where water is heated to generate steam. The steam produced by both types of boilers is used for various industrial applications, making them essential equipment in the manufacturing and processing industries.</p></p>
