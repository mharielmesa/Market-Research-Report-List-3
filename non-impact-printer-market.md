<p><p>Non-impact printers are categorized into several market types based on their technology and functionality. Thermal printers use heat to produce images on paper. Electrostatic printers utilize static electricity to transfer toner onto paper. Laser electrostatic printers use a laser to create an electrostatic image on a photosensitive drum. Inkjet printers spray tiny droplets of ink onto paper. Thermal transfer printers use heat to transfer ink onto paper. Each of these market types offer different features and advantages for various printing needs.</p></p>
