<p><p>Aluminum-clad cables are categorized into three main types based on the type of metallic sheath used: Smooth Metallic Sheath, Corrugated Metallic Sheath, and Other. Smooth Metallic Sheath cables have a smooth outer layer for easy installation and protection. Corrugated Metallic Sheath cables are designed with a ridged outer layer for added durability and strength. The Other category includes cables with unique sheathing materials or designs that do not fall within the smooth or corrugated classifications. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
