<p><p>There are two main types of car tyre markets: radial and bias. Radial tyres are made with belts running at right angles to the tread, providing better traction and fuel efficiency. They are more commonly used in modern vehicles. Bias tyres, on the other hand, have belts running at 30-45 degree angles to the tread, offering better stability and load-bearing capacity. They are typically found in older vehicles or specific applications such as off-road driving. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on the vehicle's needs and usage.</p></p>
