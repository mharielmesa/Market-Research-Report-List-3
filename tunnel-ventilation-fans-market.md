Jet fans, on the other hand, are preferred for shorter tunnels due to their ability to direct airflow in specific directions. Both types of fans play a vital role in maintaining air quality, controlling temperature, and preventing the buildup of harmful gases in tunnels, ensuring a safe environment for both vehicles and pedestrians.</p></p>
