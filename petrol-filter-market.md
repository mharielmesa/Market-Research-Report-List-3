<p><p>The petrol filter market is divided into two main types: carburetor and EFI (Electronic Fuel Injection). Carburetor filters are designed for traditional engines that rely on a carburetor to mix air and fuel, whereas EFI filters are suited for modern engines equipped with electronic fuel injection systems. Both types serve the same purpose of filtering out impurities and ensuring clean fuel reaches the engine, but they are tailored to meet the specific requirements of different engine technologies.</p></p>
