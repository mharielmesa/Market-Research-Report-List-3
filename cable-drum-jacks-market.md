<p><p>Cable drum jacks are used in the cable industry for lifting and moving heavy cable drums. There are three main types of cable drum jacks - hydraulic, mechanical, and manual. Hydraulic jacks use fluid power to lift and lower the drum, making them efficient and easy to operate. Mechanical jacks utilize gears and levers to lift the drum, offering a more traditional approach. Manual jacks require physical effort to lift the drum, making them suitable for smaller and lighter applications. Each type caters to different needs in the market.</p></p>
