<p><p>Multilayer varistors are widely used in the automotive market in two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Multilayer Varistors and Through Hole Multilayer Varistors. SMD Multilayer Varistors are surface-mounted components that provide protection against voltage spikes and surges in automotive electronics. Through Hole Multilayer Varistors are inserted into holes on circuit boards and offer similar protection. Both types of multilayer varistors are crucial in ensuring the reliable operation of automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
