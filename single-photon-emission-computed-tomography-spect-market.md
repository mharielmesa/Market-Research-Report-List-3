There are two main types of SPECT market: single imaging gamma cameras and SPECT/CT. Single imaging gamma cameras are standalone devices that are used for SPECT imaging, while SPECT/CT combines SPECT with computed tomography to provide more detailed and precise images. This combination allows for better localization and characterization of abnormalities in the body.</p></p>
