<p><strong>The Hair Restoration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Extraction</li><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair Restoration market offers various types of treatments for hair loss, including Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT). FUE involves individually extracting hair follicles from the donor area and transplanting them to the recipient area, resulting in a natural-looking hairline. FUT, on the other hand, involves removing a strip of scalp from the donor area and dissecting it into individual follicular units before transplanting them. Both treatments are effective in restoring hair for individuals experiencing baldness or thinning hair.</p></p>
