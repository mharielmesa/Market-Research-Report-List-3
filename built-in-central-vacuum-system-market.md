<p><p>The built-in central vacuum system market can be divided into two main types: Centralized Type and Distributed Type. </p><p>Centralized Type systems feature a central unit typically located in a garage or utility room, with pipes running throughout the house to connect to various vacuum inlet points. </p><p>On the other hand, Distributed Type systems consist of multiple smaller units placed strategically throughout the house, each with its own suction power and collection bin. </p><p>Both types offer convenience and efficiency in cleaning, with centralized systems being more suitable for larger properties and distributed systems for smaller spaces.</p></p>
