<p><p>Mid-infrared ultrafast fiber lasers are divided into two main market types: continuous wave (CW) and pulse. Continuous wave lasers emit a continuous beam of light, ideal for applications such as spectroscopy and material processing. On the other hand, pulse lasers emit light in short bursts, enabling precise cutting and processing in fields like micromachining and medical procedures. Both types of lasers have their own unique advantages and are widely used across various industries for a range of applications.</p></p>
