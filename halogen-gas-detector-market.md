<p><p>Halogen gas detectors are designed to detect the presence of halogen gases in various environments to ensure safety. There are two main types of detectors: electrochemical and infrared. Electrochemical detectors utilize a chemical reaction between the gas and an electrolyte, producing an electrical signal to indicate gas concentration. Infrared detectors, on the other hand, use a light source to analyze the absorption of infrared radiation by the gas molecules. Both types of detectors are commonly used in industrial settings to monitor and prevent halogen gas leaks.</p></p>
