<p><p>Refrigeration dehumidifiers come in three main types: Normal Type, Heat Pipe Type and New Air Type. Normal Type dehumidifiers work by drawing air into the unit, cooling it to remove moisture, and then releasing the drier air back into the room. Heat Pipe Type dehumidifiers use a special heat exchange system to remove humidity from the air. New Air Type dehumidifiers use a combination of refrigeration and desiccant technology to effectively remove moisture from the air. Each type offers unique features and benefits for controlling humidity levels in different environments.</p></p>
