<p><p>The standalone large format display market consists of three main types - LED-backlit LCD, OLED, and E-Paper. LED-backlit LCD displays use light-emitting diodes to provide backlighting for liquid crystal displays, offering bright and vivid colors. OLED displays use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is passed through them, resulting in high contrast ratios and energy efficiency. E-Paper displays use electronic ink technology to mimic the appearance of traditional paper, making them easy on the eyes and suitable for reading for extended periods.</p></p>
