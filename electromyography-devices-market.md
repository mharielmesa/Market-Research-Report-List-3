<p><p>Electromyography devices market can be divided into two main types: stationary EMG devices and portable EMG devices. Stationary EMG devices are large, immobile units typically found in hospitals or clinics for conducting detailed and comprehensive muscle assessments. On the other hand, portable EMG devices are smaller, more compact units that can be easily carried and used in different settings, allowing for convenient on-the-go muscle monitoring and analysis. Both types serve the same purpose of measuring and recording electrical activity in muscles, but cater to different needs and environments.</p></p>
