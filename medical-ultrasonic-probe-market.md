<p><p>The Medical Ultrasonic Probe market consists of four main types: Linear, Convex, Phased Array, and Endocavitary. Linear probes are best suited for superficial imaging, while Convex probes are used for abdominal and pelvic imaging due to their curved design. Phased Array probes are ideal for cardiac and vascular imaging, offering a smaller footprint. Endocavitary probes are designed for internal examinations such as gynecological or urological scans. Each type caters to specific medical imaging needs, providing healthcare professionals with versatile tools for diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
