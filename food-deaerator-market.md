<p><p>The Food Deaerator market consists of three main types: Spray-tray Type, Vacuum Type, and Spray Type. Spray-tray Type deaerators remove oxygen by spraying hot water over a series of trays. Vacuum Type deaerators operate by creating a vacuum to remove dissolved gases from the liquid. Spray Type deaerators use a combination of spraying and vacuum technology to efficiently deaerate food products. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various applications within the food industry.</p></p>
