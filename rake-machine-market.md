<p><p>Rake machines are commonly categorized into two main types based on their operational orientation: horizontal and sideways markets. Horizontal rake machines are designed to operate in a linear or straight direction across the field, making them suitable for larger, open fields with minimal obstacles. On the other hand, sideways rake machines are characterized by their ability to move in a lateral or side-to-side manner, making them more suitable for smaller or irregularly shaped fields with tighter spaces. Both types of rake machines offer specific advantages depending on the field's characteristics and layout.</p></p>
