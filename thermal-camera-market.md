<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal cameras can be categorized based on the wavelength they use to detect heat. Short-wave length cameras operate in the infrared spectrum, typically used for long-range imaging. Mid-wave length cameras are more sensitive to temperature differences and are commonly utilized in industrial applications. Long-wave length cameras are effective for detecting heat from a distance, often used in security and surveillance systems. Each type of thermal camera offers unique capabilities and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
