<p><p>Medical nebulisers are devices used to deliver medication in the form of mist for respiratory conditions. There are three main types of nebulisers on the market: Pneumatic nebulisers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist, Ultrasonic nebulisers use vibrations to create the mist, and Mesh nebulisers use a vibrating mesh to aerosolize the medication. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, such as speed of delivery, noise level, and size of the mist particles produced.</p></p>
