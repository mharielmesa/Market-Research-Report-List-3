<p><p>Chemical pump market consists of various types including centrifugal pump, positive displacement pump, diaphragm pump, and turbine pump. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used due to their simple design and high efficiency in moving large amounts of fluids. Positive displacement pumps are suitable for handling high viscosity fluids and precise flow control. Diaphragm pumps are ideal for handling corrosive and abrasive fluids. Turbine pumps are known for their high efficiency in transferring fluids at high pressures. Each type offers unique benefits for different chemical pumping applications.</p></p>
