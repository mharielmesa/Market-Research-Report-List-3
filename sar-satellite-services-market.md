<p><p>SAR satellite services operate in three main market segments based on frequency bands: L-band, C-band, and X-band. L-band SAR satellites provide global coverage and are ideal for applications requiring wide coverage and lower resolution, such as maritime surveillance and disaster monitoring. C-band SAR satellites offer improved resolution and are suitable for urban planning and agriculture monitoring. X-band SAR satellites provide the highest resolution and are commonly used for military surveillance and infrastructure monitoring. Each frequency band caters to specific needs and requirements in the SAR satellite services market.</p></p>
