<p><strong>The Galvanized Iron Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless</li><li>Welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized iron pipes are available in two main types: seamless and welded. Seamless galvanized iron pipes are made without any welded seams, providing a smooth and uniform surface. They are commonly used in high-pressure applications where strength and precision are critical. Welded galvanized iron pipes, on the other hand, are made by welding together pieces of metal to create a strong and durable pipe. They are often used in applications where cost-effectiveness is a priority. Both types of pipes are commonly used in construction, plumbing, and industrial settings.</p></p>
