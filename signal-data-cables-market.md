<p><p>Signal and Data Cables market types include Twisted Pair (Copper), Coax (Copper), and Optic Cables (Fiber). Twisted Pair cables are cost-effective and commonly used for Ethernet connections. Coax cables are known for their high bandwidth and are often used for television and internet connections. Optic cables, made of fiber optic material, are preferred for long-distance and high-speed data transmission due to their capacity to carry large amounts of data with minimal interference. Each type of cable offers specific advantages and is used in different applications based on their characteristics.</p></p>
