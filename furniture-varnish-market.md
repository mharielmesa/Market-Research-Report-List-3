<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Furniture varnish market is segmented into waterborne and solventborne types. Waterborne varnishes are eco-friendly, low in VOCs, and dry quickly. They are suitable for indoor furniture. Solventborne varnishes are durable and resist scratches and chemicals, making them ideal for outdoor furniture. However, they are high in VOCs and have a longer drying time. The waterborne varnish market is growing due to increasing environmental concerns, while the solventborne varnish market remains popular for its durability and suitability for outdoor use.</p></p>
