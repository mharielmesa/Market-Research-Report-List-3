<p><p>Plastic doctor blades are widely used in various printing applications such as flexo, gravure, and offset printing. In flexo printing, doctor blades are used to remove excess ink and ensure precise ink transfer onto the substrate. In gravure printing, they help maintain consistent ink flow and achieve high-quality prints. In offset printing, plastic doctor blades assist in preventing ink build-up on the printing plates. Additionally, they are also utilized in other printing processes to optimize ink distribution and enhance overall print quality.</p></p>
