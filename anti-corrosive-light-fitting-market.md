<p><p>Anti Corrosive Light Fittings are classified into two main types: Single Batten and Twin Batten. Single Batten fittings consist of a single tube light housed in a protective casing, while Twin Batten fittings include two tube lights within the casing. Both types are designed to resist corrosion and are commonly used in industrial and outdoor settings where exposure to moisture, dust, and other elements may cause traditional light fittings to deteriorate quickly. These fittings provide durable and long-lasting lighting solutions for challenging environments.</p></p>
