<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) Equipment Market includes Roadside Units (RSUs) and On-Board Units (OBUs). RSUs are installed along toll roads to communicate with vehicles equipped with OBUs, which are typically mounted on a vehicle's windshield. RSUs collect toll fees from OBUs through radio frequency identification technology, allowing for seamless, contactless toll payments. The market for RSUs and OBUs is constantly evolving, with advancements in technology leading to improvements in efficiency and convenience for both toll road operators and users.</p></p>
