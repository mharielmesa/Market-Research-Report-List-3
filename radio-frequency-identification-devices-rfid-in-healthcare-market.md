<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification Devices (RFID) in Healthcare Market consists of three main types: RFID Tags, RFID Readers, and Systems & Softwares. RFID Tags are physical labels that store data and transmit it wirelessly to RFID Readers. RFID Readers are devices that read the data from RFID Tags. Systems & Softwares are the digital infrastructure that manages the data collected by RFID Tags and Readers. Together, these components form a comprehensive solution for tracking and managing assets, patients, and inventory in healthcare settings.</p></p>
