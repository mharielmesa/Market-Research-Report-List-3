<p><ul><li>PEMFC</li><li>DMFC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hydrogen fuel cell market is classified into three main types: Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell (PEMFC), Direct Methanol Fuel Cell (DMFC), and others. PEMFC utilizes a polymer membrane as an electrolyte, offering high efficiency and power density. DMFC operates on methanol and is commonly used in portable applications due to its simplicity. The "others" category includes Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC) and Alkaline Fuel Cells (AFC) which are still in the development stages for automotive use.</p></p>
