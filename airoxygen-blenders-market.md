<p><p>Air/Oxygen Blenders are medical devices used to mix oxygen and air to deliver a specific oxygen concentration to patients. They are categorized into low-flow (0-3 lpm bleed flow), mid-flow (3-6 lpm bleed flow), and high-flow (6-12 lpm and above) types based on the amount of oxygen and air that can be blended. Low-flow blenders are suitable for patients with mild respiratory issues, mid-flow for moderate conditions, and high-flow for severe respiratory conditions requiring higher oxygen levels.</p></p>
