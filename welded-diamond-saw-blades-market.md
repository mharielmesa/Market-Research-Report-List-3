<p><p>Welded Diamond Saw Blades market is primarily divided into two types- High-frequency Welding and Laser Welding. High-frequency Welding involves using high-frequency electric current to weld the segments to the steel core, resulting in a strong bond. On the other hand, Laser Welding utilizes laser technology to weld the segments to the core, offering precise and clean cuts. Both types of welding methods are popular in the market for their durability and efficiency in cutting various materials such as concrete, stone, and metal.</p></p>
