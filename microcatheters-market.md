<p><p>Microcatheters are small, thin and flexible tubes used in various medical procedures. They are categorized into different markets based on their intended use. Delivery microcatheters are used to deliver devices or drugs to specific locations in the body. Diagnosis microcatheters are used for imaging and identifying abnormalities within the body. Aspiration microcatheters are used to remove fluids or tissues from the body. Steerable microcatheters have the ability to navigate through complex vascular structures with ease. Each type of microcatheter serves a specific purpose in medical interventions.</p></p>
