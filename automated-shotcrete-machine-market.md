<p><strong>The Automated Shotcrete Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Shotcrete</li><li>Dry Shotcrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Shotcrete Machine market is segmented into two main types: Wet Shotcrete and Dry Shotcrete. Wet Shotcrete involves mixing water with the dry mix before spraying, resulting in a slurry that is sprayed onto the surface. It is more commonly used for applications requiring high strength and durability. Dry Shotcrete, on the other hand, involves spraying the dry mix onto the surface and then adding water through a nozzle. It is typically used for applications requiring quick setting times and where water contact is limited.</p></p>
