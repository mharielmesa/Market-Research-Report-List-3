<p><ul><li>Edge Emitting Laser Chip</li><li>Surface Emitting Laser Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor laser chips are categorized into two main types: edge emitting laser chips and surface emitting laser chips. Edge emitting laser chips emit light from the edge of the chip, allowing for higher power output and longer distances. Surface emitting laser chips, on the other hand, emit light from the surface of the chip, offering more compact and efficient designs for applications such as optical communications and sensing. Both types of semiconductor laser chips play crucial roles in various industries such as healthcare, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
