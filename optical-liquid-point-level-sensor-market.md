<p><strong>The Optical Liquid Point Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical liquid point level sensors come in two main types - contact and contactless. Contact level sensors use physical contact with the liquid to determine its level, while contactless sensors rely on optical technology such as infrared or ultrasonic waves to detect the level without touching the liquid. Contact sensors are often more accurate but may be subject to wear and tear, while contactless sensors are more convenient and durable, making them suitable for a wider range of applications.</p></p>
