<p><ul><li>Wet Heat Bain Maries</li><li>Dry Heat Bain Maries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Bain Maries are categorized into two main types based on the heating method: Wet Heat Bain Maries and Dry Heat Bain Maries. Wet Heat Bain Maries use water to create steam for indirect heat cooking, making them ideal for keeping food warm and maintaining moisture levels. On the other hand, Dry Heat Bain Maries use heating elements to provide direct heat, making them suitable for cooking and holding dry foods at specific temperatures. Both types cater to different culinary needs in commercial kitchens.</p></p>
