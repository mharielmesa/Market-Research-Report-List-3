<p><ul><li>Flaps</li><li>Spoilers</li><li>Slats & Slots</li><li>Leading Edge Flaps</li><li>Vortex Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft lift control devices market types include flaps, spoilers, slats & slots, leading edge flaps, and vortex generators. Flaps are hinged surfaces on the trailing edge of the wing that increase lift at slower speeds. Spoilers disrupt airflow over the wing to decrease lift. Slats & slots are movable surfaces on the front of the wing that improve lift at low speeds. Leading edge flaps modify the curvature of the wing to increase lift. Vortex generators create small vortices to enhance lift and control.</p></p>
