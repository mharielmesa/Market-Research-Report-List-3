<p><p>Cold rolled low carbon steel drum barrels can be categorized into two main types based on their design: open head steel drums and closed head steel drums. Open head steel drums have a removable top lid, allowing for easier access to the contents inside. On the other hand, closed head steel drums have a fixed top lid that is typically secured with a bolt or clamp. Both types of drum barrels are commonly used for storage and transportation of various liquids and solids in industrial applications.</p></p>
