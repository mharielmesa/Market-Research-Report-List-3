<p><p>Textiles enzymes are categorized into various types based on their specific functions in the textile industry. Cellulase enzymes are used for biopolishing and biofinishing of cotton fabrics. Amylase enzymes help in removing starch-based sizing agents from fabrics. Catalase enzymes are used for bleaching processes. Pectinase enzymes aid in the removal of pectin from textile fibers. Laccase enzymes contribute to the coloration and modification of textiles. Other types of enzymes may include proteases, lipases, and peroxidases with various applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
