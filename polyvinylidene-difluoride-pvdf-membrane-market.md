<p><p>Polyvinylidene Difluoride (PVDF) Membrane is available in two main types: Hydrophobic PVDF Membrane and Hydrophilic PVDF Membrane. Hydrophobic PVDF Membrane repels water while allowing the passage of organic solvents, making it ideal for applications involving non-aqueous solutions. On the other hand, Hydrophilic PVDF Membrane attracts water, ensuring high wettability and making it suitable for use in aqueous solutions. Both types of PVDF membranes offer unique properties that cater to specific filtration needs in various industries.</p></p>
