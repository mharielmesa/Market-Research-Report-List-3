<p><p>Soil thermometers are classified into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I soil thermometers are designed for measuring temperatures in the upper layer of soil, typically up to a depth of 10 cm. These thermometers are suitable for gardening and agricultural applications. On the other hand, Type II soil thermometers are designed for measuring temperatures at greater depths, usually up to 100 cm. These thermometers are more commonly used for scientific research and environmental monitoring purposes.</p></p>
