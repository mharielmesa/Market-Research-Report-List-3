<p><p>Wardrobe hinges come in two main types: concealed hinges and visible hinges. Concealed hinges are hidden from view when the wardrobe door is closed, providing a sleek and seamless look to the furniture. They are popular for modern, minimalist designs. Visible hinges, on the other hand, are mounted on the outside of the wardrobe door and are more traditional in style. They offer a classic look and are often chosen for antique or rustic furniture styles.</p></p>
