<p><p>A heat transfer plate, also known as a heat exchanger plate, is a component used in heat exchangers to transfer heat between two fluids. Additionally, the focus on energy efficiency and environmental sustainability is also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the heat transfer plate market is the growing adoption of compact heat exchangers that require smaller footprint and offer higher heat transfer efficiency.
