<p><p>The Safety Belt Alarm Harness market consists of three main types: Slide Version, Mechanical Version, and Hall Version. Slide Version harnesses have sensors that detect movement and tension changes in the belt. Mechanical Version harnesses have a built-in alarm system that is triggered when the belt is not in use. Hall Version harnesses use Hall sensors to detect when the belt is not fastened. Each of these types offers unique features to ensure the safety of passengers and drivers when using safety belts.</p></p>
