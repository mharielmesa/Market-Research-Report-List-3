<p><p>Four Quadrant Photoelectric Sensors are classified into two types - Four Quadrant PIN Photoelectric Sensor and Four Quadrant APD Photoelectric Sensor. The Four Quadrant PIN Photoelectric Sensor operates using a PIN diode which offers fast response times and low noise levels, making it suitable for high-speed applications. On the other hand, the Four Quadrant APD Photoelectric Sensor utilizes an avalanche photodiode for higher sensitivity and wider detection range, making it ideal for low-light environments and long-range applications. Both sensors cater to different market needs based on their unique features and advantages.</p></p>
