<p><p>Carton flow rack systems are categorized into various types based on the design of the conveying mechanism. Roller lanes type use rollers to move cartons smoothly along the rack. Wheel beds type utilize wheel beds for easy movement of cartons. Tilted shelving type involves shelves that are tilted to allow gravity to move cartons down. Wheel rails type have rails with wheels attached for carton movement. Other types may include variations or combinations of these designs to suit specific storage needs.</p></p>
