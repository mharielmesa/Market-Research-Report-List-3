<p><p>Semi-skimmed fish meal market includes flame dried (FD) and steam dried (SD) fish meal types. Flame dried fish meal is produced by cooking and drying fish at high temperatures, resulting in a high protein content and rich nutritional profile. Steam dried fish meal involves steaming the fish before drying, preserving more of the natural oils and nutrients. Both FD and SD fish meal are commonly used as ingredients in animal feed due to their protein content and nutritional benefits.</p></p>
