<p><p>Oil based defoamers are classified into two main types: mineral oil based defoamers and polyether based defoamers. Mineral oil based defoamers are derived from petroleum sources and are commonly used in applications where hydrophobicity is required. On the other hand, polyether based defoamers are made from synthetic polymeric materials and are known for their excellent foam control properties. Both types of defoamers are widely used in various industries such as food processing, textiles, and wastewater treatment to suppress foam formation and improve process efficiency.</p></p>
