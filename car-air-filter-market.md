<p><p>The car air filter market offers two main types: paper filters and oil bath filters. Paper filters are made of pleated paper material that effectively traps dirt and debris to prevent them from entering the engine. On the other hand, oil bath filters use oil to trap particles and contaminants. While paper filters are more commonly used in modern vehicles due to their efficiency and ease of replacement, oil bath filters are durable and require less frequent maintenance. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and efficiency of the engine.</p></p>
