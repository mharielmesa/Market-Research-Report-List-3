Evaporative Coolers and Atomizers use the process of evaporation to cool the incoming air, making it more comfortable for occupants. Chillers and Mechanical Refrigeration systems utilize refrigeration technology to lower the temperature of the air before it enters a building or facility. Both types of systems are crucial in providing efficient and effective cooling solutions for various industries and applications.</p></p>
