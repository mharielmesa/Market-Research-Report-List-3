<p><p>District Cooling Energy System Market offers three main types of cooling technologies: Free Cooling, Absorption Cooling, and Compression Cooling. </p><p>Free Cooling utilizes ambient air or water to cool the system without the need for additional energy consumption. Absorption Cooling uses a refrigerant absorption cycle powered by waste heat or other low-grade heat sources. Compression Cooling relies on mechanical compression of refrigerant gases to provide cooling. Each technology offers unique advantages in terms of energy efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact in the district cooling market.</p></p>
