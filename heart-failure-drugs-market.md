<p><p>The Heart Failure Drugs Market can be categorized into different types based on their mechanism of action. These types include ACE inhibitors (ACEIs), Angiotensin II Receptor Blockers, ARNIs, Beta Blockers, Aldosterone Antagonists, and Diuretics. ACEIs and ARNIs work by blocking the effects of certain hormones that can worsen heart failure. Angiotensin II Receptor Blockers also act on these hormones. Beta Blockers help to reduce the workload on the heart, while Aldosterone Antagonists and Diuretics help to reduce fluid retention in the body.</p></p>
