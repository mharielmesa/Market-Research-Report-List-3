<p><p>Nylon drag chains are commonly classified into two main types based on their structure: open type and closed type. Open type drag chains have an open structure, allowing for easy access to cables and hoses within the chain. On the other hand, closed type drag chains have a fully enclosed structure, providing better protection for cables and hoses against external elements. Both types cater to different industry needs, with open type being more suitable for applications requiring frequent maintenance and closed type for applications demanding maximum protection.</p></p>
