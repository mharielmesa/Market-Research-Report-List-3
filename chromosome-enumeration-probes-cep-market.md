<p><p>Chromosome Enumeration Probes (CEP) market includes three main types: Single Color Probes, Dual Color Probes, and Multi-color Probes. Single Color Probes are used to target specific chromosomes with one fluorescent dye, providing clear and easy-to-interpret results. Dual Color Probes use two different fluorescent dyes to detect specific chromosomal abnormalities, offering higher accuracy. Multi-color Probes allow for the simultaneous detection of multiple chromosomal abnormalities by using multiple fluorescent dyes. Each type of probe offers unique advantages and applications within the chromosome enumeration market.</p></p>
