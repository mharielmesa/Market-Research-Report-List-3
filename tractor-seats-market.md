<p><p>There are two main types of tractor seats available in the market: Mechanical Suspension Seats and Air Suspension Seats. Mechanical Suspension Seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks and provide a more comfortable ride for the operator. On the other hand, Air Suspension Seats use compressed air to adjust the seat height and provide a smoother ride even on rough terrains. Both types of seats are designed to reduce operator fatigue and improve overall comfort during long hours of operation.</p></p>
