<p><ul><li>Smooth Rotor</li><li>Slotted Rotor</li><li>Drilled Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive brake rotors come in three main types: Smooth rotors provide consistent braking performance and durability. Slotted rotors have slots cut into the surface to help disperse heat and gas buildup for improved braking under heavy load. Drilled rotors have holes drilled into them to further aid in heat dissipation and prevent brake fade. Each type of rotor has its own benefits and is suitable for different driving conditions, making it important to choose the right type for optimal braking performance.</p></p>
