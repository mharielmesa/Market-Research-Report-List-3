<p><p>Furniture panels market offers various types of panels, each with unique features and uses. Particleboard (PB) is affordable and versatile, suitable for a wide range of furniture. Medium density fibreboard (MDF) is denser and more durable, a popular choice for cabinets and shelving. High density fibreboard (HDF) is even stronger and used for heavy-duty furniture. Oriented strand board (OSB) is engineered for strength in structural applications. Plywood is a versatile and strong option made from thin layers of wood veneer, suitable for high-quality furniture.</p></p>
