<p><p>Linear triblock copolymer market types are determined by the method of polymerization used to produce the copolymer. In the active anionic polymerization market, the polymerization process is carried out using a living polymerization technique, resulting in precise control over the polymer structure and properties. Conversely, in the inactive anionic polymerization market, the polymerization process involves the use of chain transfer agents or terminating agents, which may result in less control over the final copolymer structure and properties.</p></p>
