<p><p>Fibre optic cables are widely used in telecommunications and networking for high-speed data transmission. There are two main types of fibre optic cables on the market: single-mode optical cables and multimode optical cables. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission with a single light mode, making them ideal for large-scale networks. Multimode cables, on the other hand, are suitable for shorter distances with multiple light modes, making them cost-effective for smaller networks or local connections. Both types are crucial for building efficient and reliable communication systems.</p></p>
