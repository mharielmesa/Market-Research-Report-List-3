<p><p>Rare Hematology Market includes two main types of treatments - Recombinant Factors and Plasma Derived Factors. Recombinant Factors are biotechnology-derived clotting factors that are produced in a lab. They are considered safer and more effective than traditional plasma-derived factors. On the other hand, Plasma Derived Factors are derived from human plasma and are used to treat bleeding disorders. Both types of treatments play a crucial role in managing rare hematological disorders and improving the quality of life for patients with these conditions.</p></p>
