<p><p>Adhesiveless Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) market is divided into two main types: Single-sided Non-adhesive FCCL and Double-sided Non-adhesive FCCL. </p><p>Single-sided Non-adhesive FCCL consists of a single layer of copper foil bonded directly to a flexible substrate without any adhesive layer in between. This type is commonly used in applications where flexibility and lightweight are primary considerations.</p><p>Double-sided Non-adhesive FCCL features copper foil layers on both sides of the flexible substrate, without any adhesive bonding the layers together. This type offers enhanced flexibility and conductivity, making it suitable for more complex electronic designs and applications.</p></p>
