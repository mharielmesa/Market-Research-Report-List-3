<p><p>PECVD systems are used for depositing thin films on substrates in various industries. There are three main types of PECVD systems: RF-PECVD, ECR-PECVD, and MW-PECVD. RF-PECVD uses radio frequency waves to ionize gases and deposit thin films. ECR-PECVD utilizes electron cyclotron resonance to create plasma and deposit films. MW-PECVD employs microwave energy for plasma generation. Each type of PECVD system has its own set of advantages and is used for specific applications based on the requirements of the industry.</p></p>
