<p><ul><li>Suction Filters</li><li>Return Oil Filters</li><li>High Pressure Line Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic oil filters serve different purposes in the market. Suction filters are used to prevent contaminants from entering the system during fluid intake. Return oil filters help in removing contaminants before the fluid re-enters the reservoir. High pressure line filters are designed to withstand high pressure levels and ensure the cleanliness of fluid during transmission. Each type plays a critical role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of hydraulic systems by removing impurities and protecting components from wear and damage.</p></p>
