<p><p>PV Metallization Aluminium Paste market can be classified into two main types based on the solar cell technology they are used in: BSF (Back Surface Field) Solar Cells and PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) Solar Cells. BSF solar cells utilize a back surface field to reduce recombination losses, while PERC solar cells have a passivated emitter and rear cell structure to enhance light absorption and efficiency. Both types of cells rely on metallization aluminum paste for conducting electricity and facilitating electron flow within the solar cell.</p></p>
