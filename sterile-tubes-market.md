<p><ul><li>Rounding Bottom Sterile Tubes</li><li>Conic Bottom Sterile Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterile tubes are essential for various laboratory procedures, with two common types being rounding bottom sterile tubes and conic bottom sterile tubes. Rounding bottom sterile tubes have a smooth, curved bottom surface, making them ideal for mixing liquids and preventing sedimentation. In contrast, conic bottom sterile tubes have a tapered bottom shape, which facilitates easy removal of samples and helps in efficient centrifugation. Both types cater to different requirements in the laboratory setting, providing researchers with options for their specific needs.</p></p>
