<p><p>Infertility drugs market can be categorized into three main types: Gonadotropins, Anti-Estrogens, and Others. Gonadotropins are hormones that stimulate egg production in women and sperm production in men. Anti-Estrogens work by blocking the effects of estrogen in women, helping with ovulation. Other types of infertility drugs include aromatase inhibitors, which lower estrogen levels, and selective estrogen receptor modulators, which modulate estrogen activity. These drugs are used to treat various causes of infertility and help improve chances of conception.</p></p>
