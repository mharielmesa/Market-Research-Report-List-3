<p><p>Portable moisture analysers are available in three main types: Microwave, Infrared, and Loss on Drying (LOD). Microwave analysers use microwave technology to measure moisture levels quickly and accurately. Infrared analysers use infrared light to determine the moisture content of a sample. LOD analysers involve heating a sample to evaporate moisture and then measuring the weight loss. Some other types of portable moisture analysers may use different technologies or methods to analyze moisture content in a sample.</p></p>
