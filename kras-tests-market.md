<p><p>KRAS tests are used to detect mutations in the KRAS gene, which plays a role in cancer development. There are three main types of KRAS tests available in the market. KRAS Sanger sequencing tests analyze DNA to identify specific mutations in the gene. KRAS Nucleic Acid Amplification Tests (NAAT) use techniques like PCR to amplify and detect mutated DNA. Other KRAS tests include next-generation sequencing methods or immunohistochemistry. These tests help doctors determine the most effective treatment for patients with KRAS-mutated cancers.</p></p>
