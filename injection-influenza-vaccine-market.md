<p><p>Injection Influenza Vaccine Market consists of two main types - Trivalent Influenza Vaccine and Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine. Trivalent Influenza Vaccine protects against three strains of influenza virus - two influenza A strains and one influenza B strain. Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine, on the other hand, protects against four strains of influenza virus - two influenza A strains and two influenza B strains. Both types of vaccines are administered via injection and are widely used to prevent influenza infections and outbreaks.</p></p>
