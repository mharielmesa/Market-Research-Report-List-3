<p><p>Dehydrated foods market is categorized into various types such as Spray-dried foods, Freeze-dried foods, Vacuum-dried foods, and others. Spray-dried foods involve the process of converting liquid or paste-like food products into dry powder form. Freeze-dried foods are created by freezing the food product and then removing the ice through sublimation. Vacuum-dried foods involve removing moisture from the food product under low pressure. Other types may include air-dried foods and sun-dried foods, which utilize different methods to remove moisture from the food product.</p></p>
