<p><p>Structural adhesives are crucial in various industries for bonding substrates, providing high strength and durability. The market is segmented into five major types: Epoxy, Polyurethane, Acrylic, Methyl Methacrylate, and Cyanoacrylate. Epoxy adhesives are known for their excellent bonding properties, while Polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and impact resistance. Acrylic adhesives provide fast curing and high transparency. Methyl Methacrylate adhesives offer rapid curing and excellent weather resistance. Cyanoacrylate adhesives are popular for their fast setting and high strength bonding capabilities.</p></p>
