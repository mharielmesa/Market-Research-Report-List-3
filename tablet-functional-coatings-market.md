<p><p>Tablet functional coatings market types include cellulose polymer, vinyl derivatives, acrylic polymer, and other polymers. Cellulose polymer coatings are commonly used for their ability to modify drug release properties. Vinyl derivatives are known for their water resistance and film-forming properties. Acrylic polymer coatings offer excellent barrier protection and adhesion properties. Other polymers, such as enteric polymers, provide specific functionalities like pH-sensitive drug release. These various coating types cater to different needs in the pharmaceutical industry for efficient drug delivery and product protection.</p></p>
