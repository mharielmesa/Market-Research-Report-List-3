<p><p>Electrochemical based devices market includes various types of equipment such as titrators, electrochemical meters, ion chromatographs, potentiostats, and other related instruments. Titrators are used for measuring concentrations of chemicals in a solution, while electrochemical meters are used for measuring voltage and current in electrochemical cells. Ion chromatographs are used for separating and analyzing ions in a sample. Potentiostats are devices that control the voltage between electrodes in an electrochemical cell. Other devices in this market cater to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
