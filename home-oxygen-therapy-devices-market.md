<p><p>Home oxygen therapy devices market includes two main types: oxygen source equipment and oxygen delivery devices. Oxygen source equipment includes stationary oxygen concentrators, portable oxygen concentrators, and liquid oxygen systems. These devices extract oxygen from the surrounding air or store it in liquid form. Oxygen delivery devices include nasal cannulas, oxygen masks, and nasal tubes, which deliver the oxygen to the patient. Both types of devices are essential for managing respiratory conditions and improving the quality of life for patients requiring oxygen therapy at home.</p></p>
