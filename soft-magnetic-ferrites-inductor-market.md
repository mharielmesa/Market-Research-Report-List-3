Mn-Zn ferrite core inductors are widely used in power supply applications due to their high permeability and low core loss. Nickel-Zinc ferrite core inductors are preferred for their high saturation magnetization and low eddy current losses. Other market types include complex ferrite core compositions and niche applications requiring specific magnetic properties.
