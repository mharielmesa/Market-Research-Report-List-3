<p><p>The sterile empty vials market includes various types such as sterile evacuated vials, sterile nitrogen filled vials, and sterile air filled vials. Sterile evacuated vials are sealed under vacuum to prevent contamination, while sterile nitrogen filled vials have nitrogen gas inside to displace oxygen and maintain product integrity. Sterile air filled vials, on the other hand, are filled with filtered air to maintain a neutral atmosphere. Each type serves a specific purpose in pharmaceutical and healthcare industries for storing and transporting sensitive materials.</p></p>
