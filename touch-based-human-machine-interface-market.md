<p><p>Touch based human machine interfaces come in various types such as resistive, capacitive, surface acoustic wave, and others. Resistive touch screens work by detecting pressure, capacitive touch screens use electrical fields to sense touch, and surface acoustic wave technology relies on ultrasonic waves to register touch. Other types may include infrared and optical touch screens. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of sensitivity, durability, accuracy, and cost, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
