<p><p>Hydrogel coatings for medical devices can be categorized into two types: Thermal Heat Cure and UV Cure. Thermal Heat Cure coatings are activated through heat to create a strong bond with the device surface, providing durability and biocompatibility. UV Cure coatings use ultraviolet light to cure and create a thin, even layer on the device, offering quick curing time and precise application. Both types of coatings are commonly used in the healthcare industry to enhance the performance and safety of medical devices.</p></p>
