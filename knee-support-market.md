<p><p>Knee support products are broadly categorized into two types: rigid and soft. Rigid knee supports are designed to provide maximum support and stability to the knee joint during strenuous activities or post-injury rehabilitation. They are made of rigid materials such as metal or hard plastic. On the other hand, soft knee supports are made of flexible materials like neoprene or fabric, offering compression and mild support for minor knee discomfort or as a preventive measure during low-impact activities. Both types cater to different needs and degrees of knee support.</p></p>
