<p><p>The car auto dimming mirror market can be divided into two types: inside auto dimming mirrors and outer auto dimming mirrors. Inside auto dimming mirrors are located inside the vehicle and automatically adjust based on the amount of light received, reducing glare from headlights behind the car. Outer auto dimming mirrors are located on the exterior of the vehicle and help improve visibility by reducing glare from headlights of vehicles approaching from the rear. Both types of mirrors enhance driving safety by minimizing distractions caused by bright lights.</p></p>
