<p><p>Thermoelectric battery market consists of two main types: Single Stage Module and Multiple Modules. Single Stage Module uses only one module to convert heat into electricity, making it simpler and more cost-effective for smaller-scale applications. On the other hand, Multiple Modules market involves connecting several modules together to increase power output, making it more suitable for larger-scale applications requiring higher energy conversion efficiency. Both types offer unique advantages and are utilized based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
