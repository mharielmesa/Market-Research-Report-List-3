<p><p>Semiconductor Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) market can be categorized into two main types: Wafer Testing and Package Testing. Wafer Testing involves testing the electrical characteristics of semiconductor wafers before they are cut into individual chips. Package Testing, on the other hand, focuses on testing the final packaged semiconductor devices to ensure they meet quality and performance standards. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and functionality of semiconductor products before they are released into the market.</p></p>
