<p><p>Spine devices market includes spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are used to join two or more vertebrae together to treat conditions such as spinal deformities or injuries, while non-fusion products provide dynamic stabilization or motion preservation without fusing the vertebrae. The demand for spinal fusion products is driven by the increasing prevalence of spinal disorders, while non-fusion products are preferred for their ability to maintain natural movement in the spine. Both segments contribute to the growth of the spine devices market.</p></p>
