<p><p>Architectural White Marble market is divided into two main types - Natural Marble and Artificial Marble. Natural Marble is extracted from quarries and is valued for its unique veining and natural beauty. It is known for its durability and high-end appeal. Artificial Marble, on the other hand, is a man-made product created using a combination of natural materials and resins. It offers a more uniform appearance and is often more cost-effective than natural marble. Both types cater to different market segments based on preferences and budget constraints.</p></p>
