<p><p>Educational microscopes are categorized into three main types: optical microscopes, electron microscopes, and scanning probe microscopes. Optical microscopes use visible light to magnify samples, making them suitable for basic educational purposes. Electron microscopes use a beam of electrons to achieve higher magnification and resolution, making them ideal for advanced scientific studies. Scanning probe microscopes create detailed images by scanning the surface of a sample with a tiny probe, making them useful for nanoscale research and education.</p></p>
