<p><p>Liquid chromatographic columns market consists of two main types - HPLC (High Performance Liquid Chromatography) columns and UHPLC (Ultra High Performance Liquid Chromatography) columns. HPLC columns are used for routine analytical separations, while UHPLC columns offer higher resolution and faster separations due to smaller particle sizes and higher pressure capabilities. HPLC columns are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing, while UHPLC columns are gaining popularity in research settings for their improved efficiency and sensitivity.</p></p>
