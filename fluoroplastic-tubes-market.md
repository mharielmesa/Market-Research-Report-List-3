<p><ul><li>PTFE Tubes</li><li>PFA Tubes</li><li>FEP Tubes</li><li>ETFE Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoroplastic tubes are categorized into four types: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) tubes, PFA (perfluoroalkoxy) tubes, FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene) tubes, and ETFE (ethylene tetrafluoroethylene) tubes. PTFE tubes are known for their high temperature resistance and chemical inertness. PFA tubes offer similar properties to PTFE but with improved flexibility. FEP tubes are known for their excellent clarity and low friction. ETFE tubes provide high strength and durability along with resistance to abrasion and chemicals. These various types cater to different industrial applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
