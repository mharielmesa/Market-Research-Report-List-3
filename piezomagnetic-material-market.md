There are three main types of piezomagnetic materials in the market: Metal piezomagnetic materials, which are characterized by high magnetostriction and high permeability, Ferrite piezomagnetic materials with low losses and high magnetic permeability, and Rare Earth piezomagnetic materials, which have high magnetocrystalline anisotropy and high remanent magnetization. These different types cater to specific applications and industries based on their unique properties.</p></p>
