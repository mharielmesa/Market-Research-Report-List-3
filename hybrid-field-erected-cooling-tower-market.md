<p><strong>The Hybrid Field-Erected Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Draft</li><li>Forced Draft</li><li>Induced Draft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid field-erected cooling towers are classified into three main types based on their draft system: natural draft, forced draft, and induced draft. </p><p>Natural draft cooling towers rely on natural convection currents for the circulation of air. Forced draft cooling towers use mechanical fans to draw air through the tower, while induced draft cooling towers use fans located at the top of the tower to pull air upwards. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as energy efficiency, maintenance requirements, and environmental impact.</p></p>
