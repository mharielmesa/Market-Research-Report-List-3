<p><strong>The Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbidimetric</li><li>Chromogenic</li><li>rFC Assays</li><li>Gel Clot</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endotoxin testing market encompasses various types of assays including turbidimetric, chromogenic, rFC assays, gel clot, and others. Turbidimetric assay measures the change in turbidity when endotoxins react with lysate. Chromogenic assay uses chromogenic substrates that change color in the presence of endotoxins. rFC assays use recombinant Factor C protein for endotoxin detection. Gel clot assay involves observation of gel formation in the presence of endotoxins. Other assays may include fluorescence-based or enzyme-based methods for endotoxin detection.</p></p>
