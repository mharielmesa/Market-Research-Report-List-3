<p><p>Air lubrication systems are innovative technologies that reduce friction between a ship's hull and the surrounding water, increasing fuel efficiency and reducing environmental impact. There are two types of air lubrication systems: AirFilms and MicroBubble. AirFilms create a thin layer of air between the hull and water, while MicroBubble systems inject tiny air bubbles under the hull to reduce drag. Both systems have been proven to significantly decrease a ship's energy consumption and emissions, making them valuable solutions for the maritime industry.</p></p>
