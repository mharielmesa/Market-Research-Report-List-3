<p><p>Inferior Vena Cava (IVC) Filters are divided into two main types: retrievable and permanent. Retrievable IVC Filters are designed to be implanted temporarily and removed once the risk of blood clots has diminished, providing a temporary solution. Permanent IVC Filters, on the other hand, are designed to remain in the body long-term to prevent blood clots from traveling to the lungs. Both types of filters serve the purpose of preventing pulmonary embolism in patients at risk of blood clot complications.</p></p>
