<p><p>Residential humidifiers can be categorized into two main types: vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers work by heating water to create steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity levels. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers use a fan or ultrasonic vibrations to disperse water droplets into the air. Both types of humidifiers are commonly used in homes to combat dry air and improve overall indoor air quality.</p></p>
