<p><p>Ultra-violet fluorescent ink is used for security and authenticity purposes in various sectors. Invisible ultraviolet fluorescent ink is not visible under normal lighting conditions but glows brightly under UV light, making it ideal for covert markings. Visible ultraviolet fluorescent ink, on the other hand, is visible both under normal lighting and UV light, offering versatility in applications where the markings need to be visible at all times. Both types of UV fluorescent ink cater to different security needs and are widely used in industries such as banking, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
