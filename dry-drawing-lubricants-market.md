<p><p>Dry drawing lubricants are commonly categorized into three main types: sodium soap based lubricants, calcium soap based lubricants, and other lubricants. Sodium soap based lubricants are formulated using sodium soap, which provides excellent lubricating properties. Calcium soap based lubricants, on the other hand, are based on calcium soap and are known for their high performance in drawing operations. The "other" category includes lubricants that do not fall under sodium or calcium soap based lubricants, such as synthetic lubricants or blends of different types of lubricants. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
