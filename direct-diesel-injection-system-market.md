<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Direct Diesel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Unit Injector Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Diesel Injection System market consists of three main types: Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System, Unit Injector Injection System, and Common Rail Injection System. The Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System uses separate pumps to pressurize the fuel and deliver it to each injector. The Unit Injector Injection System combines the pump and injector into one unit per cylinder. The Common Rail Injection System utilizes a high-pressure fuel rail to supply fuel to all injectors simultaneously. Each system has its own distinct advantages and applications in the diesel engine industry.</p></p>
