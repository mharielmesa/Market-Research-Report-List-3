<p><ul><li>Drum Compost Turners</li><li>Elevating Face Compost Turners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum Compost Turners are commonly used in small to medium scale composting operations. They consist of a rotating drum that mixes and aerates the compost material, promoting decomposition. Elevating Face Compost Turners are ideal for larger-scale operations, as they have a larger capacity and can cover a greater area. They feature a conveyor belt system that lifts and mixes the compost material, ensuring efficient turning and aeration. Both types of turners are essential for accelerating the composting process and producing high-quality compost.</p></p>
