<p><p>Magnesium Carbonate Minerals Market includes two main types: Magnesite and Hydromagnesite. Magnesite is a natural ore that is found in deposits around the world and is commonly used in industries such as refractories, chemicals, and construction materials. On the other hand, Hydromagnesite is a hydrated form of magnesium carbonate that is typically produced synthetically by reacting magnesium-rich solutions with sodium carbonate. It is used in industries such as flame retardants, environmental protection, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
