<p><p>Spray dried urea-formaldehyde (UF) can be classified into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I UF is characterized by low formaldehyde content, making it suitable for applications where low emissions are required, such as in indoor furniture and cabinetry. On the other hand, Type II UF has a higher formaldehyde content, making it more suitable for applications where durability and moisture resistance are important, such as in exterior wood products. Both types offer different properties and are used in specific applications based on the requirements.</p></p>
