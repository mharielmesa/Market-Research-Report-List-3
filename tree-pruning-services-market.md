Thinning involves removing excess branches to improve air circulation and reduce density. Raising focuses on trimming lower branches to enhance visibility and provide clearance for buildings or vehicles. Reduction involves reducing the size of the tree by cutting back branches to maintain its shape and size. Each type of pruning service caters to different needs and preferences of customers, ensuring the health and aesthetics of trees.</p></p>
