<p><strong>The Payroll Outsourcing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Managed Outsourcing</li><li>Co-Managed Outsourcing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full-Managed Outsourcing refers to a type of payroll outsourcing where a company transfers all payroll functions to a third-party service provider. The service provider takes complete responsibility for managing all aspects of the payroll process, including payroll calculations, tax filing, and compliance. Co-Managed Outsourcing, on the other hand, involves sharing the responsibilities of payroll processing between the company and the service provider. In this arrangement, the company retains control over certain aspects of payroll while outsourcing others to the service provider.</p></p>
