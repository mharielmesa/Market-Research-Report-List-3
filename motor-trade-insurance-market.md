Third Party coverage provides protection against claims made by third parties for injury or damage caused by the insured's vehicle. Third Party Fire and Theft includes coverage for theft or fire damage in addition to third party claims. Comprehensive coverage is the most inclusive, providing protection for third party claims, theft, fire damage, and damage to the insured vehicle. Each market type offers different levels of protection for motor traders.</p></p>
