<p><p>Polymer Additive Manufacturing (AM) Machines come in several types, including FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling), SLA (Stereolithography), DLP (Digital Light Processing), MJ (Material Jetting), SLS (Selective Laser Sintering), and other markets. FDM machines use thermoplastic filaments, SLA and DLP utilize liquid resin cured by light, MJ machines jet materials onto a build platform, and SLS machines use a laser to sinter powdered material. Each type offers unique capabilities and cater to different applications in the additive manufacturing industry.</p></p>
