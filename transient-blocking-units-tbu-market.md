There are two main market types for TBUs: Bidirectional TBUs, which can block transients in both directions, and Unidirectional TBUs, which only block transients in one direction. Both types help to prevent damage to sensitive electronic components by redirecting dangerous surges away from the equipment. These TBUs are essential for maintaining the reliability and longevity of electronic systems.</p></p>
