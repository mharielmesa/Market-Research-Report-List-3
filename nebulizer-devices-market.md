<p><p>Nebulizer devices are commonly categorized into three types based on their mechanism of action: Pneumatic Nebulizers, Ultrasonic Nebulizers, and Mesh Nebulizers. Pneumatic Nebulizers use compressed air or oxygen to create a fine mist of medication. Ultrasonic Nebulizers use high-frequency vibrations to produce aerosol droplets. Mesh Nebulizers create aerosol droplets by vibrating a mesh with tiny holes. Each type of nebulizer has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
