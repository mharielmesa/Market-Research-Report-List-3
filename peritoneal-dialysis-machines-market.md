<p><p>Peritoneal Dialysis Machines come in two main types: Power Type and Gravity Type. Power Type machines use an automated system to pump dialysis solution into and out of the abdomen, making the process more efficient and precise. Gravity Type machines rely on the force of gravity to fill and drain the abdomen with the dialysis solution, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types of machines are used in clinics and at home for patients who require peritoneal dialysis treatment.</p></p>
