<p><p>Microfluidics technology market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used in the manufacturing process. Silicon-based microfluidics involve the use of silicon wafers to create microchannels and structures. Glass-based microfluidics utilizes glass materials for similar purposes. Apart from these, there are other markets in microfluidics that may involve unconventional materials or combinations of different materials for specific applications.</p></p>
