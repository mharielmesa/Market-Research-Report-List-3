<p><p>Cardiology Catheters are used to diagnose and treat heart conditions through the insertion of a thin tube into the heart. Guidewires assist in navigating catheters to the desired location within the heart. Balloon Catheters are used to widen narrowed arteries by inflating a balloon at the blockage site. Coronary Stents are small mesh tubes inserted to keep arteries open after balloon angioplasty. Fractional Flow Reserve is a measurement used to assess the severity of coronary artery blockages. Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) is a diagnostic tool using soundwaves to create images of blood vessels.</p></p>
