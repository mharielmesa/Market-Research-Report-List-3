<p><p>Commercial Aircraft Landing Gear & Undercarriage Market Types include Strut, Rocker, Framed, and Pontoon. Strut landing gear consists of a single leg attached to the aircraft structure, providing support during landing and taxiing. Rocker landing gear has a larger wheelbase to provide stability during ground operations. Framed landing gear features multiple legs attached to a frame for enhanced support and shock absorption. Pontoon landing gear is designed for amphibious aircraft, with inflatable floats to enable water landings. Each type caters to specific aircraft design and operational requirements.</p></p>
