<p><p>Nasoenteric feeding tubes are a type of medical device used to provide nutrition and medication to patients who are unable to eat or swallow. There are three main types of nasoenteric feeding tubes: nasogastric feeding tubes, nasojejunal feeding tubes, and nasoduodenal feeding tubes. Nasogastric feeding tubes are inserted through the nose and into the stomach, while nasojejunal feeding tubes are inserted further down into the jejunum, and nasoduodenal feeding tubes are inserted into the duodenum. Each type of tube is used based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
