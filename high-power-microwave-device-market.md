<p><p>High Power Microwave Devices Market is divided into two main types: Active Microwave Devices and Passive Microwave Devices. </p><p>Active Microwave Devices are electronic components that require an external power source to operate, such as amplifiers, oscillators, and switches. These devices are capable of generating and controlling microwave signals.</p><p>Passive Microwave Devices, on the other hand, do not require an external power source and include components like antennas, couplers, and filters. These devices are used for signal routing, coupling, and filtering in microwave systems.</p></p>
