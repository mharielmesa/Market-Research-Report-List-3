<p><p>Integrated centrifugal concentrators can be classified into two main types based on the presence of a rotor: concentrators with rotor and concentrators without rotor. Concentrators with rotor utilize a spinning rotor to separate particles based on their density, while concentrators without rotor rely on other mechanisms such as fluid dynamics to achieve the same outcome. Both types of concentrators are used in various industries for the efficient separation and concentration of materials.</p></p>
