<p><ul><li>GC Chromatography</li><li>Frontal Chromatography</li><li>Adsorption-Desorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Elemental Analyzer market consists of three main types: GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography involves separating and analyzing elements based on their interactions with a gas chromatograph. Frontal Chromatography uses a stationary phase to separate and detect elements in a sample. Adsorption-Desorption relies on the adsorption and desorption processes to separate and identify elements in a sample. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in elemental analysis.</p></p>
