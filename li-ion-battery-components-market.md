<p><p>The Li-ion Battery Components Market consists of four main types: cathode, anode, electrolyte, and separator. The cathode is the positive electrode, the anode is the negative electrode, the electrolyte is the conductive medium that allows for the flow of ions between the cathode and anode, and the separator is the insulating material that physically separates the cathode and anode while allowing for the passage of ions. These components are essential for the proper functioning of a Li-ion battery.</p></p>
