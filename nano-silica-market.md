<p><strong>The Nano Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano silica market can be divided into two main types: P type and S type. P type nano silica is produced through vapor-phase hydrolysis and is known for its high purity and specific surface area. It is commonly used in electronics, optical devices, and catalysts. On the other hand, S type nano silica is produced through liquid-phase hydrolysis and is characterized by its larger particle size and lower specific surface area. It is often used in construction materials, rubber products, and cosmetics.</p></p>
