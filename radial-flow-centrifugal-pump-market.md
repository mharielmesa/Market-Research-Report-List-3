<p><p>Radial flow centrifugal pumps are classified into three main types based on their design and operation: Single-stage pump, Two-stage pump, and Multi-stage pump. </p><p>Single-stage pumps have a single impeller and are suitable for applications requiring low to medium pressure. Two-stage pumps have two impellers and are ideal for applications that demand higher pressure. Finally, multi-stage pumps incorporate multiple impellers in a series to deliver even higher pressure levels for challenging industrial applications.</p></p>
